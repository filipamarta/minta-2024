Minta & The Brook Trout

Oh, falling in love. Like everything else, it’s different as you get older, isn’t it? As you get stuck in your ways, and other people get stuck in theirs too?

This is a love song. A song about making room for someone when you’re both lived long enough to have had your heart broken over and over.

A duet with Shelley Short, a wonderful singer-songwriter from the US who is currently living in Australia.





Recorded by Nelson Carvalho, assisted by Tiago Correia, at louva-a-deus, in Lisbon Portugal mixed and mastered by Eduardo Vinhas (Golden Pony). additional recordings by Mariana Ricardo and Francisca Cortesão at louva-a-deus. Shelley Short recorded by Ande Foster and Georgia Rose at Gympie Flexi Studio in Gympie QLD, Australia.

Do you know the feeling of being overwhelmed by sensing that whatever you create, be it almost automatically or with all your heart, will be swallowed by the algorithm?

That's what random information is about: yet, hopefully, you can dance to it.
